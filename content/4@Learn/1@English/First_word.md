#### Establish 

---

**意思：**

<details>
	<p>
    建立，奠定，打下基础，确定
  </p>
</details>

**常考：**

* 确认，证实

生产力的革命是否确有其事，这很难确认

<details>
  <p>
    What is harder establish is whether the productivity revolution is for real.
  </p>
</details>

其中的因果关系还没有得到证实

<details>
  <p>
    A direct causal relationship has not yet been establish
  </p>
</details>

* 有根基的，有基础的，已确立的  **(established)**

> 老生意伙伴                                     established business partners
>
> 明确的趋势                                    well- established trend
>
> 已确立的规范                                established conventions 



<font color="red">替换</font> `established` `to get start in life`

#### Shift

---

<details>
	<p>
    v.转移，变换 n.改变，转移
  </p>
</details>

**常考：**

* 转移

在自我怀疑与盲目骄傲之间来回切换

<details>

  </p>
</details>

我们的焦点应该转移到社会福利上

<details>
  <p>
    Our focus should shift to community welfare.
  </p>
</details>

* 放慢生活节奏 **（Downshifting）**





<font color="red">替换</font> `shift` `switch`

#### Expect

</details>

>
> 频繁的突击秘密调查                                   	  frequent unexpected secret checks
